# Factual Data Verification

## Purpose
Verify that supply chain relationships in the data are **factually true** - backed by actual company disclosures, SEC filings, and official announcements.

## Methodology
1. Sample 20 companies across different ranks/sectors
2. For each company, verify 3-5 key supply chain claims
3. Check against primary sources (SEC 10-K, official press releases, annual reports)
4. Mark each claim as: ✅ Verified | ⚠️ Partial | ❌ Unverified
