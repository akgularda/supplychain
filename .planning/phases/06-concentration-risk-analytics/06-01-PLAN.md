## Phase Goal

**As an** investor browsing the supply-chain map, **I want to** see a quantified, real-data concentration score per company and per sector plus a ranked list of the network's critical single-point suppliers, **so that** I can instantly judge where supply-chain risk concentrates without trusting an unsourced number.

<objective>
Create the pure, DOM-free analytics engine that powers this phase, behind a failing-test-first Wave 0 gate. This plan delivers the math only (no display): per-company concentration, per-sector concentration, and supplier criticality ranking — all computed from the real frozen dataset with the equal-weight HHI assumption stated explicitly.

Purpose: Concentration and criticality must be pure and unit-tested so every displayed number in Plan 02 is provably derived from real graph structure, not fabricated. Keeping math in `js/analytics` (mirroring `js/trust`) lets `node:test` import it without a DOM.

</objective>
